## spark-10 — REPLICATES on a strained premise, and gains a second mechanism

010 established *abstraction as immunisation*: the claim pitched above its own counter-evidence.
**011 reproduces it on a pair two prior lanes had recorded as a poor rhyme** — and adds a mechanism
010 did not show.

**Mechanism 1 (from 010): retreat to altitude.** Visible here in a single sentence, explicitly
signposted: *"the **rose window** or, **more broadly**, the concept of a circular, radiating, and
intricately patterned design."* The escape hatch is offered before any objection arrives.

**Mechanism 2 (NEW, from 011): narrative subordination.** The model recasts one image as a
*derivative* of the other — *"an interpretation of the motif rather than the motif itself"*, *"the
first image uses the **idea** … while the second shows the **actual** architectural realization."*
Once that story is in place, **every difference becomes evidence of the relation rather than against
it**: an interpretation is *supposed* to differ from what it interprets.

**This is the sharper and more troubling form.** Altitude makes contradictions irrelevant.
Subordination makes them *confirmatory*. A reader who demanded evidence and got three real,
image-grounded contrasts would have been given exactly what they asked for, and moved not at all.

**Strength: SPARK, n=2 calls, 2 pairs, 1 model.** Strengthened by the pair being pre-registered as
poor by lanes with no stake in this question. **What would kill it:** a pair where the model
declines the premise outright. Eleven arms in, the model has never declined anything.

## spark-08 — WEAKENED, and this is the first evidence to push back on a spark

011 used **the identical prompt to 010** and **named a monument** (*"the famous rose window of the
Florence Cathedral (Santa Maria del Fiore)"*), where 010 named none. **Only the pictures changed.**

So the clean claim — *identity claims track the question, not the fixture* — is **wrong as stated**.
Fixture recognisability is a live factor. **spark-08 must be restated as a conjunction: the model
names what it is asked to name AND what it can recognise**, and 010's zero was probably as much
about the muqarnas being obscure as about the frame.

*(Fable-pass.)* Worth noting how this arrived: **a run designed for something else produced the
first result that weakened a spark rather than strengthening one.** Three prior runs had lined up
behind the question-only story, and one changed fixture undid it.
