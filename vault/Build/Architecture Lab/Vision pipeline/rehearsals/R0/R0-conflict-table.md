## Net correction

The earlier PASSAGE-CIRCULATION plan is a plausible *vertical-slice hypothesis*, but three of its
gates (percept-in-writer, return, roles) sit on top of **partly-existing, unexercised** machinery,
and two (Atlas, Codex) sit on **absence**. The Research Program's instruction ‚Äî run diverse
rehearsals first, let repetition choose the slice ‚Äî is the correct next move: it prevents building
`Passage`/`Embodiment` schemas to fit one eloquent run before the machinery that already exists is
even shown to fail.
