
A Level Up from Social Media

This is a product dedicated towards the fashion and entertainment culture. The culture comprises both of the creative industry and the audience.
The project is dedicated for both of them.

An upliftment from the traditional dominant source of entertainment and fashion- Instagram dominated medium.
We dont aim to replace the medium. Perhaps it is a very good medium, led to a platform creation where fashion and entertainment at a very feasible handy way could be created and consumed. 
It led to consumption of the artform. But mere consumption renders the thing as dopamine food to be consumed.
But the amount of musical, fashionable, comical, literary and several forms created on social media platforms like instagram, youtube, tiktok, twitter deserve more than being a consumed dopamine food.

something which treat and cherish their sophistication, artform-ness. A Taste and story layer
