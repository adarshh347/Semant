**4. Empty, reading, and editing now share the same frame**  
Before, empty state, reading state, and edit state behaved like separate layouts.

Now they are meant to feel like different states of the same story column:

```
Story frame├── identity/meta-head├── empty OR reading OR editing body└── tags/meta-foot
```

Purpose: reduce layout jump and help the user feel they are always in one story surface.
