# Purpose Structure Surface

The three axes we judge every component on, in order.

1. **Purpose** — what is it *for*? Does it serve the job of the page?
2. **Structure** — is it the fewest components, each with one home? (architecture)
3. **Surface** — how does it look? Colour, spacing, feel. (last)

Rule: **Purpose decides Structure decides Surface.**

For [[Drishya and Semant]] the Purpose is: *turn one image into meaning — read it, mark its parts, write its story (by hand and with Sutradhar), while the reader can trace who saw what and who wrote what.*

Test any element with three questions: does it serve that purpose? is it the leanest structure? (surface: later). An element that fails Purpose is removed or moved, never just restyled. Related: [[Constraints we must not break]].
