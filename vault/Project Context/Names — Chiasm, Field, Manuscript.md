# Names — the Chiasm lexicon (LOCKED)

The workspace where you see an image and write from it. Sanskrit initiative names (Darshan, Aletheia, Anuraṇana) stay as *concept/section* names; the product surfaces get this English lexicon.

| Surface | Name | Meaning / why | Replaces |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The combined workspace** (image + read + write) | **Chiasm** | Merleau-Ponty — the intertwining/crossing of the *seer and the seen*, the visible and the said. It literally names Field × Manuscript. | Atelier · Drishya · `PostDetailPage` · Sutradhar |
| **The visual pane** (image, regions, reading) | **Field** | the visual field; where the image and its parts are seen/marked | the left/Visual pane |
| **The writer pane** (block editing) | **Manuscript** | the writing surface; BlockNote-powered; also usable standalone | Loom · Writer · the right/Content pane |
| **AI authored content marker** | (keep `origin`/`actor`) | provenance stays a data field, not a brand name | "Sutradhar" as the AI's name is retired |

Notes:
- **Manuscript** is one thing in two mounts: inside **Chiasm** (beside Field) and **standalone** (compose without an image). Same BlockNote editor, one persistence contract.
- Routes (later rename, not now): `/chiasm/:postId`, `/manuscript` (standalone). Keep `/posts/*` until the rename pass.
- "Sutradhar" (the AI writer name) is dropped; the trace of AI authorship lives in block `origin`/`actor`.
